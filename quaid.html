Who is Quaid E Azzam?

Muhammad ali jinnah was born in December 25,1876 Karachi,Pakistan and died in september 11 1947 in Karachi,Pakistan

Quaid E Azzam Or Muhammad Ali Jinnah was a man again imperial rule anti colonist and a Politian statesmen and the founder of Pakistan in the British Raj, He led the Pakistan movement, which was successful in gaining Pakistan's independence.

He was the first governor-general of the Dominion of Pakistan until his death.
